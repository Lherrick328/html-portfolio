The Best Movies According to Lenny

My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time

Saving Private Ryan

This is my all time favorite movie. It does an amazing job showing how brutal WWII actually was and had amzing acting.

The Raid 2

This is my favorite martial arts movie. The fight scenes are choreographed to perfection, and were some of the best I have even seen in a film.

Logan

This is probably one of the best comic book movies ever. I loved Wolverine growing up and this movie really captured the raw emotion and grittiness surrounding Logan as a character.
